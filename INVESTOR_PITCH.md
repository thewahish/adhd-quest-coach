## Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Low Conversion from Free to Paid
**Mitigation:**
- Value-packed free tier to build trust
- Clear upgrade path with meaningful features
- AI coaching as premium hook
- Testimonials and case studies

### Risk 2: High Churn
**Mitigation:**
- Built-in habit formation mechanics (streaks, leveling)
- Regular check-ins and engagement
- Community features for accountability
- Flexible system that adapts to bad days

### Risk 3: AI API Costs
**Mitigation:**
- Tiered AI usage (free tier limited)
- Smart caching and optimization
- Negotiate volume discounts with Anthropic
- Hybrid approach (rules + AI)

### Risk 4: Competition
**Mitigation:**
- First-mover advantage in ADHD gamification
- Strong community moat
- Continuous innovation
- Network effects through social features
